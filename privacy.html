The fine print

Privacy

We make videos about your right to privacy, so we take it seriously here too. The short version: we collect as little as possible, use no tracking cookies, and never sell your data.

What we collect

  • Newsletter email. If you sign up for the Tuesday & Thursday digest, your email address is stored by our newsletter provider, Buttondown, solely to send you the digest. You can unsubscribe from any email at any time.
  • Topic suggestions. If you use the "Suggest a topic" box, your message is delivered to us through Formspree so we can read it. Don't include sensitive personal information.
  • Anonymous, cookieless analytics. We use privacy-friendly analytics that count page views without cookies and without collecting personal data or building a profile of you. There is no cross-site tracking.

Video embeds

Episodes are embedded from YouTube using its privacy-enhanced (no-cookie) mode, so YouTube does not set tracking cookies unless you press play. Once you play a video, YouTube's own privacy policy applies to that playback.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your information to anyone.
  • We don't use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.
  • We don't try to identify you across other websites.

Your choices

Unsubscribe from the newsletter using the link in any email, or ask us to delete your address. For anything else, just email us.
